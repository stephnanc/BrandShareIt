Understanding the Metrics

Sales (Sell-In)

What it measures: Revenue from shipments to UNFI/retailers

When recorded: When product leaves our warehouse

What it tells us: Our revenue and market expansion

Our performance: +43% growth

Velocity (Sell-Through)

What it measures: Actual consumer purchases at checkout

When recorded: When product scans at retail POS

What it tells us: True consumer demand and inventory health

Our performance: +35% growth

Why This 8-Point Gap Matters

An 8-point gap between sales and velocity growth indicates healthy business expansion. We're growing distribution faster than demand, but within sustainable limits. This suggests proper promotional support, good inventory management, and strong retailer partnerships. Industry best practice targets gaps under 10 points - we're executing at that benchmark.
