Scheduling & Appointment Costs

C3 Online Scheduling System
www.c3reservations.com/KeHE
  • Separate C3 User ID required for each DC (contact receiving DC for User ID)
  • Appointment requests required at least 7 days prior to "Ship To Arrive" date
Rescheduling Fees
  • Must be done through C3 system
  • Carrier's responsibility to reschedule
  • Fees apply for rescheduling within 3 days of scheduled appointment
Small Parcel Shipments (FedEx/UPS Ground)
  • No C3 appointment needed
  • Typically a few cases, less than 150 lbs
  • Lumper fees apply
FedEx/UPS Freight
  • C3 appointment required
  • Shipments typically on pallets, over 150 lbs
  • Lumper fees apply

Additional Transportation-Related Costs

On-Time Delivery Performance Fee
  • If supplier's average On-Time Delivery rate is less than percentage specified in Inbound Routing Guide Fee Schedule during a calendar quarter, fee assessed on all late shipments in that quarter
  • Fee assessed as deduction at end of non-compliance quarter
  • This is IN ADDITION to Late or No Show fee charged to carrier at time of delivery
Expedited Shipping
  • KeHE NOT responsible for unauthorized expedited, rushed, or air freight charges
  • All such charges are supplier's or supplier's carrier's responsibility
  • Authorization for such charges must be in writing from KeHE personnel
Storage Fees New Nov 2025

VALUE-ADD: KeHE will no longer charge storage fees of $25/pallet/week for products unable to accept as inventory (overage, non-conforming, short-dated, etc.). This is now provided as a value-add to suppliers.

Cost-Saving: Administrative Allowance Program (AAP)

New & Small Supplier Program — This program can significantly reduce transportation-related costs for eligible new and small suppliers.

Program Benefits
  • 2% flat rate off-invoice allowance
  • Lumping fees WAIVED for participating suppliers
  • Exemption from fees associated with gaining distribution (setup fees, placement fees, slotting fees)
  • Consolidates multiple fees into one simplified allowance
