Executive Intelligence Summary

2025 Full Year
Revenue Trajectory
Strong Q4 finish at $44,850 — the highest quarter — representing a 72% acceleration vs Q1's $26,100. H2 outperformed H1 by over $20,000, validating distribution expansion momentum across both territories.
WFM Dominance
Whole Foods Market is the #1 channel across both territories, anchoring over 60% of combined revenue. EAS WFM-NA drove $52,400 across 38 NE locations; FDK WFM-MA drove $38,200 across 42 Mid-Atlantic doors.
Indie Channel Strength
Independent specialty retail generated $28,500+ combined. Key performers: Yarzin Sella (Cambridge), Belfast Co-op (ME), Newport Mansions (RI), and a growing VA/DC indie cluster including Mimi's Convenience and Cornercopia.
SKU Mix Insight
Core 16ct stroopwafel formats (Caramel 758401 + French Vanilla 758402) account for 77% of revenue. The Dark Choc Dipped 14ct (758403) is a $18.4K indie-channel hero. 4ct multipacks (758410/411) are an emerging WFM-exclusive growth vector, launched late 2025.
Geographic Footprint
MA-EAS covers 6 states (MA, ME, NH, VT, RI, CT) with Massachusetts as the anchor. MD-FDK covers 5 states + DC (VA, MD, PA, NJ, DC) with Virginia leading. Combined coverage spans the Northeast and Mid-Atlantic corridors.
Velocity Accounts
Top velocity accounts by order frequency: WFM Charles River (48 lines), WFM Newtonville (30+ lines), WFM Arlington VA (42 lines), and WFM Vienna VA (40 lines). These high-frequency buyers signal strong shelf placement and consistent consumer pull-through.

Core 16ct Dominance
SKUs 758401 and 758402 (the Caramel and French Vanilla 16ct core stroopwafels) together account for $110,300 — 76.7% of all revenue. These are the true workhorses of the line, stocked universally across WFM, independents, Roche Bros, and NCG co-ops.
Dark Choc Dipped — Indie Champion
The 758403 Dark Choc Dipped 14ct generates 93% of its revenue through EAS indie/specialty accounts. Near-absent in FDK (only $1,200). This is a premium-positioning SKU that thrives in natural food stores, farm stands, and specialty cheese shops — not WFM.
4ct Multipack — Rising Star
SKUs 758410 and 758411 (Caramel + FV 4ct GF multipacks) launched mid-2025 via WFM exclusively. Combined $15,150 revenue with strong FDK pull — FDK WFM-MA accounts actually drive more 4ct revenue than EAS, suggesting stronger fit with the DC/VA/MD consumer profile.
SKU Mix Recommendation
For 2026 planning: Lead with 758401/402 everywhere. Push 758403 harder into EAS independents and new FDK indie doors. Invest in 4ct multipack growth at WFM with sampling support. Watch for 758404/405 (new 12ct format) transition indicated in late 2025 order notes — these will carry the future core lineup.
