What “Velocity” Means in This Report

Velocity here is defined as units per store per week and dollars per store per week for each chain. It answers a simple question: “Once SWOFFLE is on shelf, how fast does it move?”

∙ Units Per Store Per Week (UPSPW) – average number of units sold per store, per week
∙ Dollars Per Store Per Week – average sales dollars per store, per week
∙ Percent Change – year-over-year change in those velocity metrics

Velocity removes the distortion of store count and shows true consumer pull. High velocity with low distribution signals upside. Low velocity with high distribution signals risk or a need for support.
