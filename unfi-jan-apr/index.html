4. Summary – How to talk about the 16-week YOY story

  • Overall: The last 16 weeks show +15% in sales and +23% in cases, driven primarily by top 50 chains and new/expanding distribution.
  • Top 50 chains: Strong growth from accounts like Palo Alto Egg, URM, Raise The Bar, NCGA, Super 1, Bristol Farms, Big Y, and several regional banners. A small set of chains (Festival Foods, NCG Central/East, Town & Country Montana, Barbur) are down and represent targeted opportunity areas.
  • Independents: A handful of independents are performing well, but the segment overall is down, with many accounts either significantly reduced or at zero shipments. Losses are concentrated in specific groups and former strong accounts (Natural Retail Group, Fresco Community Markets, Exporters, INFRA Atlantic, various co-ops).
  • Action lens:
    • Protect and build on the top 50 growth engines.
    • Target a short list of declining chains for reset/placement/promo conversations.
    • Prioritize high-potential independents and a focused reactivation list where business has dropped to zero but was previously meaningful.
