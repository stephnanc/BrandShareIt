Update to Shipping & Handling SOP — Small Parcel Shipments

To UNFI Vendor Ship (VSP) partners –

We're writing to share an update to UNFI's Shipping & Handling Standard Operating Procedure (SOP). This update clarifies requirements for small parcel inbound shipments sent via UPS, FedEx, or other parcel carriers.

UNFI's Shipping & Handling Policies outline inbound performance expectations to ensure product arrives at our distribution centers (DCs) on time and in the expected condition. These updates support our shared goal of improving efficiency while reducing avoidable delays, discrepancies, or costs.

Summary of Updates to Shipping: Small Package Shipments Section

Removed
  • No changes
Added
  • Each box must contain a detailed packing list. This helps ensure accurate documentation if cartons are delivered separately or if "Carton x of x" labels are missing or unclear.
  • The Purchase Order (PO) number must be entered in the reference field when creating the shipment. This assists with research and validation if transit issues occur.
Amended
  • UNFI receives UPS and FedEx shipments in bulk, with deliveries from multiple suppliers in each drop. UNFI signs only for the total number of boxes received at delivery and does not confirm receipt of cartons from individual suppliers.
  • UNFI is unable to verify or count individual cartons or items within cartons for small parcel shipments. As a result, this shipping method may increase your company's risk of product loss. UNFI is not responsible for product shortages or losses associated with small parcel shipments.

We appreciate your attention to these updates. Thank you for partnering with us to ensure a smoother, more reliable inbound delivery process across UNFI's distribution network.
