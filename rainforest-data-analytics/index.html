🚀
82 WFM Stores — Largest Footprint
With 37 stores in MA-EAS and 45 in MD-FDK, Swoffle now covers WFM's entire Northeast + Mid-Atlantic corridor. This represents one of the broadest single-distributor natural food WFM footprints in the region.
📈
MD-FDK Approaching EAS Velocity
Despite launching 15 months after MA-EAS, MD-FDK has already reached ~$2.1K/month average and is on track to surpass MA-EAS monthly pace in 2026 given larger WFM store count. Watch Q2 2026 for crossover.
📦
4ct Format Shift in Progress
MA-EAS is still 47% legacy 16pk; MD-FDK is already 72% 4ct. As 12pk (758404/405) replaces 16pk nationally and 4ct continues gaining, the combined SKU mix will likely reach 80%+ 4ct-and-under by end of 2026.
🗓
2026 on Track for Record Revenue
Jan-Feb 2026 combined pace of $12.8K for 2 months implies a ~$76K annualized run rate. If MA-EAS grows +25% and FDK grows +40% YOY, combined 2026 could reach $95–100K — a new milestone.
