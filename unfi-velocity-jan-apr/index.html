What Velocity Means

UPSPW (Units Per Store Per Week) measures how many units each store sells on average per week. A higher number means stronger consumer pull-through.

$/Store/Week translates unit velocity into dollar revenue per store per week, reflecting both movement and price realization.

% Change compares the current 16-week period (Jan 1 – Apr 23, 2026) to the same prior-year window, showing acceleration or deceleration at the store level.
