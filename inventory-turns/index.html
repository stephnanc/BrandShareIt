Understanding Cash Conversion Cycle: This measures how long your cash is tied up in operations. It starts when you buy inventory and ends when customers pay you. If your CCC is 72 days, you spend $100K today and will not see that money back for 72 days. Lower is better — faster cash return means more capital available for growth.
